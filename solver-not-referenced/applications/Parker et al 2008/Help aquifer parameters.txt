Help – Aquifer Parameters

Groundwater darcy velocity – mean groundwater darcy velocity (m/d) 

Aquifer porosity – porosity of aquifer accessible to solute by advection or diffusion (-) 

Longitudinal dispersivity – dispersivity in direction of groundwater flow (m)

Transverse dispersivity – dispersivity in horizontal direction transverse to groundwater flow (m)

Aquifer thickness – average aquifer thickness within plume extent (m)

Retardation factor – retardation factor for contaminant adsorption by aquifer solid phase (-)
